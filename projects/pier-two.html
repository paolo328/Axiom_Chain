Pier Two:
Institutional Staking Hub v2

A full institutional staking platform that lets professional clients monitor, manage and operate validator positions end to end across networks.

Stack

React, Next.js, TypeScript, Node.js

Role

UX Design, Front End Delivery

Scope

Dashboard, Validator Workflows, Rewards Reporting, API Integration

The Mission

“To give serious digital asset holders a secure, transparent and operationally mature way to stake assets without giving up custody.”

Core Objectives

  • ✓Unified visibility across validators, positions and rewards
  • ✓Non-custodial staking workflows that respect security and compliance expectations
  • ✓Reporting-oriented UX built for institutional due diligence
  • ✓API and integration surfaces that fit enterprise client journeys

The Challenge

Institutional users need more than a basic staking interface. Portfolio visibility, reward reporting and validator control must all live in one place — multi-network staking products create complexity that most dashboards handle poorly. Enterprise clients expect reliability, clarity and operational confidence at every step.

The experience must support non-custodial mental models clearly. Stake, rewards, withdrawals and reporting need to be easy to understand. The application has to work alongside APIs, custodians and wallet integrations — and UX decisions must respect security and compliance expectations without adding friction.

What We Built

Staking Hub Dashboard

Hub views for validators, positions, rewards and account status — giving institutional clients a single authoritative surface for their entire staking operation.

Validator & Lifecycle Workflows

End-to-end workflows for staking lifecycle actions and validator management, designed around operational clarity and confidence at every step.

Rewards & Portfolio Reporting

Reporting-oriented UX for institutional users — structured reward histories, portfolio views and account summaries built for professional oversight.

API & Integration Surface

Front-end systems designed to support API-connected and integration-driven client journeys, compatible with custodians, wallet providers and enterprise tooling.

Technical Architecture

A modular front-end platform where hub views, validator workflows and rewards reporting are composed into a cohesive institutional experience — backed by API integrations that connect custodians, wallet providers and account-management tooling into a single operational surface.

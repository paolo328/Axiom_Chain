The Deposit Holder:
Entire App

A digital platform for secure property deposit handling, stakeholder visibility and rapid investment / release workflows in Australian real estate transactions.

Stack

React, Next.js, Node.js, PostgreSQL

Role

Entire App

Scope

Product Strategy, UX Design, Full-Stack Application, Stakeholder Portals

The Mission

“To modernize property deposit handling with a secure, transparent and more operationally efficient digital experience.”

Core Objectives

  • ✓Replace manual, communication-heavy deposit workflows with structured digital processes
  • ✓Give all stakeholders real-time visibility into deposit status and activity
  • ✓Support investment and release workflows with confidence and auditability
  • ✓Deliver role-aware interfaces for agents, lawyers, buyers and sellers

The Challenge

Deposit handling is often manual, opaque and communication-heavy. Multiple stakeholders need updates without relying on constant back-and-forth — manual processes increase the chance of error, delay and security issues. Funds and interest handling need to feel professional, visible and tightly controlled.

The product operates in a legally sensitive environment where the experience must support very different users with very different priorities. Visibility and auditability are not optional features. The workflow needs to feel demonstrably safer than the manual process it replaces.

What We Built

Stakeholder Portal Workflows

Role-aware interfaces for agents, lawyers, buyers and sellers — each presenting the right information and actions without exposing irrelevant detail.

Real-Time Deposit Visibility

Live status tracking across deposit activity, giving all parties a single source of truth and eliminating the need for manual status updates.

Audit-Trail-Oriented Design

Every action through the handling and release workflow is recorded — creating a transparent, defensible record for legally sensitive transactions.

Investment & Release Workflows

Structured step-by-step flows for deposit investment and release actions — reducing operational risk and giving stakeholders confidence at every stage.

Technical Architecture

A full-stack platform built around stakeholder coordination and auditability — real-time deposit status flows through role-aware interfaces, with every handling and release action logged against a structured audit trail that replaces the opacity of the manual process.

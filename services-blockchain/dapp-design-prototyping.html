We take the output of discovery and turn it into something users can actually see, click through, and react to. Wallet flows, transaction journeys, on-chain interactions, all designed and tested before a line of production code is written. Our designers work alongside blockchain engineers every day, so prototypes are realistic, not aspirational.

What we design

Wallet & Authentication Flows

How users connect, authenticate, and manage wallet state. Embedded wallets vs browser extensions. The flows look very different depending on who the user is.

Transaction Journeys

Every on-chain action has a lifecycle: initiation, confirmation, pending, success, failure. We design the full journey including loading states, error handling, and gas estimation.

Token & Asset Interfaces

Balances, holdings, transaction history, rewards. How real-time on-chain data is presented without confusing latency or stale state.

Abstracted Experiences

For non-crypto users. Account abstraction, gasless transactions, social login. Designed to feel natural while keeping the on-chain benefits.

Admin & Operational Interfaces

Dashboards for protocol operators, admin panels for marketplace managers, treasury views. Often neglected early but critical for viability.

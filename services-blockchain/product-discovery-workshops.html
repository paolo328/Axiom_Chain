Product Discovery
Workshops

The most expensive mistake in product development is building confidently in the wrong direction. Discovery workshops exist to stop that.

Engagement

Workshop & Discovery

Typical Duration

1 – 5 days

Focus & Stack

WorkshopsProduct StrategyUser FlowsArchitectureRoadmapPrototyping

We compress weeks of scattered conversations, assumptions, and misaligned expectations into structured sessions that produce a delivery-ready brief. Not meeting notes. A shared foundation the entire team can build from. The framework has been refined across 50+ blockchain and AI product builds, so workshops produce plans that survive contact with engineering.

What a workshop covers

Problem & Opportunity Framing

What problem are we solving? Who has it? What does success look like commercially? We establish the strategic context before talking about features or technology.

User & Persona Mapping

Who are the actual users? Motivations, constraints, existing behaviours. For blockchain products, that includes crypto literacy levels and wallet ownership.

User Journey & Workflow Design

Core workflows walked through step by step. Every step examined for complexity, dependencies, and potential failure points.

Feature Prioritisation

Everything the product could do, ranked by impact and effort. This is where scope gets realistic and v1 gets defined.

Technical Architecture Mapping

Chain selection, contract design, API architecture, AI model selection, integrations. Enough detail to validate feasibility without locking in decisions prematurely.

Delivery Planning

Phased roadmap with milestones, dependencies, team requirements, and effort estimates.

Deliverables

What you get

  • Product brief documenting agreed scope, users, and objectives
  • User personas with motivations and constraints
  • User journey maps for each core workflow
  • Prioritised feature backlog with effort/impact scoring
  • Technical architecture document
  • Delivery roadmap broken into phases with milestones
  • Risk register with mitigations

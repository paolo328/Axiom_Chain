150 words

Axiom Chain is a Brisbane-based software development company. We build blockchain, AI and complex digital products with a senior team that handles the whole delivery, from product discovery through to engineering, smart contracts, integrations and ongoing support.

We're not a giant consultancy and we're not trying to be. We do practical work, focused on getting good products out the door without a heap of theatre around it.

Founded in 2026, we work with startups, enterprises and governments worldwide. A lot of what we get pulled into is teams that have momentum but a roadmap stretching past their internal capacity, or product complexity that needs senior hands to move cleanly.

The capability areas where we tend to fit best are blockchain (consumer dApps, DeFi protocols, smart contracts, cross-chain), AI (workflows, integrations, internal tooling) and full product development across both.

Usage guidelines

Do

  • Preserve clear-space

    Leave clear-space around the logo equal to the height of the mark — keeps it legible at any scale.

  • Match the background

    Use the dark-fill logo on light backgrounds and the white-fill logo on dark backgrounds.

  • Scale proportionally

    Resize the logo by dragging corners, not edges. Don't stretch or squish.

  • Use SVG where possible

    SVG stays crisp at any size; reach for PNG only where SVG isn't supported.

Don't

  • Don't recolour

    Use the supplied dark or light files as-is. No tints, no gradients, no outlines.

  • Don't apply effects

    No drop shadows, glows, bevels, or filters. The mark should always read as a flat shape.

  • Don't rotate or distort

    Keep the logo upright and at its native proportions.

  • Don't use old marks

    Older Axiom Chain logos found elsewhere on the web are not approved — only the files on this page are current.

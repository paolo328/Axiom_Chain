AI Audit & Opportunity
Assessment

Most organisations know AI is important. Fewer know where it’s actually useful for them. The gap between “AI could help us” and “here’s exactly where it helps, what it costs, and what it returns” is where the audit sits.

Engagement

Strategic & Advisory

Typical Duration

3 – 4 weeks

Focus & Stack

AI StrategyProcess MappingData ReadinessROI AnalysisOpportunity Assessment

A structured review of your business, processes, and data to find where AI creates real value — and where it doesn’t. The output isn’t a list of cool ideas. It’s a scored, ranked register of opportunities with effort estimates and clear next steps. Our assessors are practising AI engineers who build the systems they recommend, so effort estimates are grounded in delivery experience.

What the audit covers

Process & Workflow Mapping

Actual steps, handoffs, decision points, data inputs. For each process we check how repetitive it is, how easy the data is to access, and how much volume it handles.

Data Readiness

What data exists, where it lives, how clean it is, and who controls access. We flag gaps that need fixing before AI use cases can work.

Technology & Infrastructure Review

Current tech stack, APIs, and team skills. Can your setup handle AI workloads today, and what would need to change?

Use Case Identification & Scoring

Every AI use case scored on business impact, build effort, risk, time to value, and fit with your strategy.

Competitive Context

What peers and competitors are doing with AI. Where the industry-specific opportunities and risks sit.

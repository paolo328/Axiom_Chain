What we build

Lead Qualification Assistants

Engage prospects, ask qualifying questions, assess fit, route qualified leads to sales with full context. Work around the clock, handle multiple conversations simultaneously.

Product Discovery & Recommendation

Customer Support Assistants

First-line support connected to product knowledge and resolution patterns. Resolve common issues, gather diagnostics for complex ones, escalate with full context.

Guided Process Assistants

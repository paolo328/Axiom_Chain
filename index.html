How We Work

Our Process

Four phases. Transparent milestones. Working software every two weeks.

  1. 01

    Plan

    Before writing code, we work with you to understand the opportunity, define the product goal, align stakeholders, and reduce the risk of building the wrong thing. We help shape the roadmap, technical approach and feature priorities so the product has a strong foundation from day one.

    What happens

    • Define the product goal, users and core value proposition
    • Prioritise features into practical release stages
    • Map the high-level system architecture and integrations
    • Validate technical feasibility, risks and delivery approach
    • Identify the right tools, platforms and partners
    • Create prototypes, flows or designs where needed

    Outcome

    • ✓A clear product direction
    • ✓Aligned stakeholders and priorities
    • ✓A practical roadmap for delivery
    • ✓Tangible artefacts such as prototypes, designs or technical planning docs
  2. 02

    Build

    Once the direction is clear, our team moves into delivery with a focus on momentum, visibility and adaptability. We build in stages so you can see progress early, give feedback often, and make informed decisions as the product evolves.

    What happens

    • Dedicated specialists across front end, back end, smart contracts and cloud
    • Milestone-based delivery with regular demos and progress reporting
    • Continuous collaboration and feedback loops
    • Clear visibility over progress, blockers and trade-offs
    • Flexible involvement depending on how hands-on you want to be
    • Working features delivered incrementally, not all at the end

    Outcome

    • ✓A product that becomes usable step by step
    • ✓Faster learning through real feedback
    • ✓Clear visibility over what is built, what is next and why
    • ✓A roadmap that can evolve as the product matures
  3. 03

    Assure

    We treat quality assurance, security and technical review as part of the build, not an afterthought. From smart contract review practices to QA across the application stack, we focus on shipping with confidence.

    What happens

    • Code review and multi-level technical oversight
    • QA across core user flows and critical system behaviour
    • Automated testing where appropriate
    • Smart contract and Web3-specific review practices
    • Pre-audit preparation and internal review for blockchain systems
    • Validation of release readiness before production deployment

    Outcome

    • ✓Greater confidence in quality and security
    • ✓Clearer visibility over risks and outstanding issues
    • ✓A product that is better prepared for real users and real usage
  4. 04

    Launch

    Launching is not just a handover moment. We support the release process with the right planning, environments, deployment controls and monitoring so the product can go live safely and continue evolving.

    What happens

    • Environment setup and deployment workflows
    • CI/CD and rollback planning where needed
    • Launch planning, release checklists and communication
    • Monitoring, alerting and issue visibility
    • Post-launch support and iterative improvement planning

    Outcome

    • ✓A smoother, lower-risk release
    • ✓Better visibility once the product is live
    • ✓Confidence that the product can be supported and improved over time
    • ✓A team that stays close through launch and beyond
